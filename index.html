ABOUT

Jan Kaminsky is a technical digital creative based in Montreal, Canada, currently working at VML. With a passion for blending technology and design, Jan delivers high-quality visual and technical solutions across industries ranging from retail and CPG to health. Acting as a bridge between clients and development teams, Jan specializes in functional architecture, UX/UI, and development, ensuring seamless and impactful digital experiences.

This website serves as a curated selection of recent projects, highlighting contributions in design, functional architecture, and technical strategy. Beyond professional work, it is also a space for digital and physical art experiments, showcasing collaborations and personal creative explorations.

Whether solving complex digital challenges, crafting engaging user experiences, or experimenting with artistic concepts, Jan approaches every project with curiosity and precision. With a unique ability to merge technical expertise with creative vision, Jan brings ideas to life through thoughtful and innovative solutions.
